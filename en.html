Recreation Center "Elimai"

It is located in the territory of the Glukhovka rural district of the Beskaragai district, Abai region. The center is situated in a relict pine forest, in a unique location with special natural and climatic conditions for wellness recreation (a picturesque area, near the Irtysh River). In addition to the attractive natural and climatic conditions, the recreation center offers not only standard types of leisure but also special services such as wedding ceremonies in scenic nature spots, concerts of local and international stars, and more. Fresh air, a cozy atmosphere, friendly and attentive staff — all of this makes "Elimai" a great place for family vacations or getaways with friends. Guests are offered comfortable rooms in the hotel complex, medical supervision, cozy summer terraces, a swimming pool, gazebos, secure parking, excellent cuisine, as well as a loyalty program and discounts. The recreation center is a wonderful place for children's rest and wellness. Every year, summer and winter holidays for schoolchildren are organized here. Sports and entertainment activities in the fresh air, hardening in the pool, walks through the pine forest, trampolines, table tennis, sledding, tubing, and skiing, electric lifts — all of this allows for a full-fledged rest and wellness program for children.
